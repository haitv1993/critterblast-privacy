Privacy Policy — Critter Blast

Last updated: June 30, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how HDMT Games (“we”, “us”) handles information in the mobile game Critter Blast (the “Game”, package com.critterblast.game). By installing or playing the Game you agree to this policy.

1. Information we collect

We do not ask you to create an account and we do not knowingly collect your name, email, or phone number. The Game uses third-party services that may automatically collect limited technical data to function, show ads, and help us fix crashes:

  • Device & usage data — device model, operating system version, language, app version, and in-game events.
  • Advertising identifier — used by our ad provider to show relevant ads and measure ad performance.
  • Crash & diagnostic data — crash logs and performance data so we can improve stability.

2. Third-party services

These services may process data as described in their own privacy policies:

  • Google AdMob (advertising) — policies.google.com/privacy
  • Google Firebase Analytics (anonymous usage analytics) — firebase.google.com/support/privacy
  • Firebase Crashlytics (crash reporting) — firebase.google.com/support/privacy

3. How data is used

Collected data is used only to operate and improve the Game, display and measure advertisements, and diagnose crashes. We do not sell your personal data.

4. Children

The Game is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided such information, contact us and we will delete it.

5. Your choices

You can reset or limit your advertising identifier in your device settings (Settings → Google → Ads), and you can uninstall the Game at any time to stop all data collection.

6. Data retention & security

Data processed by the third-party services above is retained according to their policies. We take reasonable measures to protect information handled by the Game.

7. Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date above will change accordingly.
